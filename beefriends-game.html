How to Play in Real Life

Bring the Colony Under Threat experience into the classroom with this card-based role-playing game.

Materials

  • Role cards (Healthy Bee / Infected Bee)
  • Question cards
  • 5-minute timer
  • Quarantine token for each player

How to Play

  1. Players secretly draw role cards — most are Healthy Bees, 1–3 are Infected.
  2. The Beekeeper asks each bee a question. Healthy bees answer truthfully; Infected bees may hesitate or slip up.
  3. The Beekeeper nominates bees they suspect are infected.
  4. Nominated bees reveal their cards. Correctly quarantined bees are removed from the game.
  5. Win by quarantining all infected bees before time runs out!

Beekeeper Tips

  • Watch for hesitation or inconsistent answers.
  • Infected bees may avoid eye contact or change their story.
  • Ask follow-up questions to compare answers across rounds.
  • Don't quarantine too quickly — a wrongly accused bee stays in the colony!
